How to make Red Chimichuri

This red chimichuri can be used as a base for a a steak antipasto, together with caramelized onions, horseradish sauce and garlic bread

Ingredients

  • Grilled red peppers
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Red hot pepper flakes, or cayenne pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Utensils

  • Mixer
  • Colander
  • Small bowl for serving

Preparation

  1. Blend everything on high speed for 5 seconds
  2. Put the mixture in a colander, and leave it to strain for about 5 minutes
  3. Transfer to a nice serving bowl, and mix with a good splash of virgin olive oil
  4. ENJOY
